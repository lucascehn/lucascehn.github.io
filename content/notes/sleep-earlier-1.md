Ever since I went home for Christmas and agonized over layoffs, I've lost my rhythm. Not just a general rhythm in life, but my circadian rhythm.

Dumbass bitch, go to bed early. I'm literally so concerned. I'm too old to be having this problem. For the next couple of days, until the habit is formed, I'll write something before I go to bed so my bedtime is tracked. Let me game myself like Ukrainian drone teams.

+1 point to me for going to bed early.

Me points: 1

I can get 5 points max. I get -3 points if I stay up late. However, if the next day I am able to force myself to go to bed early again, I get back 3 points. And once I form the habit, me points go to another habit and I start from 0. Except, the -3 and 3 point rules still apply.

This is really annoying. I have to waste a couple days of my life on unexciting me points. But I don't trust myself to focus on two habits at once. And I guess, I nerfed literally my entire life not fixing this, so it's important even if unsexy.
